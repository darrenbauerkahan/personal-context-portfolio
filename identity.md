## What I Do

I lead product and technology for a higher-ed software company that serves school administrators and provosts. I set overall product strategy and steer execution through a product leader and several technology leaders who report to me. Day-to-day, that means directing a globally distributed organization of about 150 people through my direct reports, thinking about how to communicate with customers, and keeping my peer C-suite colleagues involved and up-to-date.

## What People Come to Me For

Connecting technology, product, and business in a way that's rare among technology leaders. I can speak to audiences of any level and size about all three — most technologists aren't aware of how a business works, what their product counterparts are trying to get done, or truly invested in how their work impacts customers. I bridge all of that together, which is what makes the CPTO role a natural fit. People come to me for strategic input that sits at that intersection, and for getting large groups of people moving in the same direction. I've managed organizations ranging from 10 to 1,000 people and have a track record of building and scaling effective teams.
