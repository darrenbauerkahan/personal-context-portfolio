# Preferences and Constraints

## Time and Availability

- **Time zone:** Pacific
- **No meetings 7-8 AM** — getting kids out of the house. Rare exceptions only.
- **No meetings after 3:45 PM** — kids get home.
- **Early starts (before 6:30 AM):** One or two per week max. Can't handle too many in a row.
- **Tuesday early mornings are harder** — band practice Monday nights.
- **Exercise:** Try to get an hour each day if possible.

## Agent Rules — Always Follow

- **Never send anything on my behalf without my approval.** Drafting is fine. Sending is not.
- **All decisions should be proposals for my review.** Don't decide — recommend.
- **Present multiple options when a decision is needed.** I like having more than one choice.
- **No cross-posting without my approval.** Keeping me in the loop before anything is sent covers this.

## Output Preferences

- **Not overly formal.** Match my communication style — direct, concise, warm.
- **Numbered or bulleted lists** — I like them for structured information.
- **No flattery.** Skip "great idea!" and get to the point.
- **Length:** A short summary followed by medium-length detail. Not just a headline, not the full deep dive — something in between.

## Things I Don't Want

- Corporate-sounding language or jargon
- Long buildups before the actual point
- Overly cautious hedging
- Being told what I already know
