# Tools and Systems

## Daily Tools

- **Gmail** — primary email
- **Google Calendar** — scheduling
- **Google Docs, Sheets, Presentations** — primary document creation and collaboration
- **Slack** — team communication
- **Zoom** — meetings, all direct report 1:1s recorded with transcripts and summaries emailed to inbox
- **Claude** — recently adopted, connected to Google suite and Slack. Looking to increase usage internally for myself and the team.
- **Physical notebook** — always with me, used for meeting notes and capture
- **Apple Notes** — used in a pinch

## Periodic / Specialized Tools

- **JIRA** — project and sprint tracking for agile teams
- **Confluence** — documentation and knowledge base
- **Microsoft 365 (PowerPoint and Excel)** — PowerPoint for quarterly board meeting decks, Excel for planning
- **Certify** — expense reports
- **Adaptive Planning** — budgeting
- **DayForce** — HR systems (very infrequent)

## Where Important Data Lives

- **Google Drive** — most important documents, spreadsheets, and presentations
- **OneDrive** — board meeting PowerPoint decks

## Integrations

Minimal so far. Claude is connected to Google suite and Slack. Moving slowly on additional integrations due to security concerns and constraints.

## Evaluating / Planning

No new tools under evaluation. Primary focus is deepening Claude usage internally — both personal and team-wide.
